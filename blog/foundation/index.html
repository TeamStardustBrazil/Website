Official founding of Team Stardust

March 11, 2026

Team Stardust was officially founded from a Minecraft Java SMP server team with the mission of bringing quality games and, moreover, treating games as they deserve to be treated: as art!

We are a small, growing team with a shared dream of creating games, and our first project is already in pre-production.

Join our Discord server to follow our projects, give suggestions, and be part of our story.

Imperial Prince

Imperial Prince is a game project currently in pre-production, promising to bring an original approach to the metroidvania genre. The story follows a young prince and his sister, whose mother—the empress and sovereign monarch—keeps them confined within the palace walls for fear that the outside world might cause them harm. Faced with this overprotection, it is up to the player to take on the role of the imperial prince and prove, through their actions, that he is strong enough to face any threat that exists beyond the royal walls.


The gameplay will combine non-linear exploration, inspired by titles such as Hollow Knight and Hollow Knight: Silksong, with progression based on the protagonist's maturation. As the prince ventures through the regions of the palace and its surroundings, he unlocks new abilities that allow him to access previously unreachable areas and uncover the secrets of the empire and his family.


Each of the prince's achievements not only expands the playable world but also reflects his personal growth—until the point where the conflict with the empress becomes inevitable, and the player will need to confront her to win their freedom. Imperial Prince aims to offer an immersive, exciting, and challenging experience for both genre fans and new players alike.
